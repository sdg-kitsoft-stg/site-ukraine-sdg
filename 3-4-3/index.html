National Metadata

Goal

3 Healthy lives and well-being
Target

3.4 Ending the HIV/AIDS and tuberculosis epidemics, including through the use of innovative practices and treatments
Indicator

3.4.3 Number of patients diagnosed with active tuberculosis for the first time, per 100,000 population
Unit of measure

Patients per 100,000 population
Organisation

State Institution “Public Health Center of the Ministry of Health of Ukraine” (Public Health Center of the MoH of Ukraine)
Comment and limitations

Includes the number of relapses of all forms of tuberculosis.
2020: A decrease in the incidence of tuberculosis in Ukraine in 2020, compared to previous years, is explained by the impact of the lockdown due to the coronavirus disease (COVID-19), caused by SARS-CoV-2.
2022–2024: Data on the resident population of Ukraine (by estimate) as of January 1, 2022 were used in the calculation.
